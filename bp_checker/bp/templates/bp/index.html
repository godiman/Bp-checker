High Blood Pressure Control

High blood pressure is also known as Hypertension.

Hypertension-WHO

Hypertension, also known as high or raised blood pressure, is a condition in which the blood vessels have persistently raised pressure.

Symptoms of hypertension

Many people with hypertension do not notice symptoms and may be unaware there is a problem. Symptoms can include early morning headaches, nosebleeds, irregular heart rhythms, vision changes, and buzzing in the ears.

Treatment

Hypertension can be managed by reducing and managing mental stress and regularly checking blood pressure.
